Resilience in the
Workplace

An interactive micro-course designed to help young professionals manage stress and navigate day-to-day challenges with composure.

Experience the Project

Roles

ID, Research, Script, Graphic Design

Tools

Articulate Rise, Krita, Canva, Figma

Audience

Young Professionals

Year

2022

The Problem

Most young professionals find it difficult to face the day-to-day challenges of work. The inability to deal with stressful situations leads to:

  • Workplace misunderstandings and fatigue
  • Inability to think clearly for creative solutions
  • Lack of control over daily tasks and dissatisfaction

Core Challenge

How can we bridge the gap in stress management for onboarding employees to ensure long-term performance and motivation?

The Solution

A dynamic micro-course implemented during the onboarding phase. This short and interactive experience hones resilience levels, allowing young professionals to face common workplace challenges with composure.
