Teachers' Mentorship

An Articulate scenario-based learning experience designed to help pre-service teachers navigate classroom challenges and build confidence through safe simulation.

Experience the Project

Role

Instructional Design, Dev

Tools

Articulate Storyline 360, JS

Audience

Pre-service Teachers

Year

2023

Project Overview

Teachers' Mentorship is a guided-learning experience designed for preservice teachers. The project leverages a scenario-based learning approach to simulate real-life interactions between a mentor and a trainee.

The goal is to help new teachers navigate the complexities of the education field, manage classroom challenges, and build confidence through mentorship simulations before stepping into a real classroom.

Mockup: Persona Card - William Wallace

The Challenge

Pre-service teachers often feel overwhelmed by practicum placements. They struggle with classroom execution and imposter syndrome. Without structured guidance, these early-career challenges can lead to burnout.

The Solution

A branching scenario simulation where learners control their journey. Interactive storytelling puts learners in the shoes of a mentee to make active decisions in a safe, risk-free environment.

Technical Features & Development

  • Branching Scenarios Promoting active decision-making through choice-driven narrative.
  • JavaScript Integration Advanced custom triggers for complex simulation logic.
  • AI-Generated Visuals Creating immersive environments and character personas.
  • Convergent Design Guiding learners through key objectives while allowing freedom.

Reflection: Looking Forward

Reflecting on this project, I would have loved to add even more complex branching paths, allowing the Pre-service Teacher to face a wider variety of distinct situations with their pupils.

However, the current model effectively demonstrates the core principles of mentorship: open dialogue, active participation, and the importance of seeking guidance to overcome self-doubt.
