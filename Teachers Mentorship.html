Portfolio / Case Study

Teachers'
Mentorship

A convergent branching scenario built to empower preservice teachers with the confidence to transition from theory to the classroom.

Experience the Project

Roles

ID, Research, Scripting, Development

Tools

Storyline 360, JavaScript, AI Visuals

Audience

Preservice Teachers

Year

2023

Project Overview

This project is a guided-learning experience created to help future educators navigate the complexities of classroom management. By leveraging a convergent scenario design, I ensured that every learner travels through a single, core narrative "stem" while having the freedom to explore various choices and see their direct consequences.

The Mentorship Dynamic

William Wallace

Mentor

Age: 42 | Senior Lecturer in Education (15+ years experience)

William has a passion for guiding preservice teachers but struggles with time management amidst his own teaching duties. He often faces resistance from students who are defensive about feedback.

Goal: To foster a collaborative environment where students take ownership of their professional growth.

Emily Garcia

Mentee

Age: 24 | Preservice Teacher (Bachelor's Student)

Emily is passionate but currently overwhelmed by the demands of her practicum and coursework. She experiences significant self-doubt and imposter syndrome when faced with setbacks in a real classroom.

Goal: To build practical knowledge and the confidence to handle diverse student needs effectively.

The Pain Point

Preservice teachers struggle to bridge the gap between educational theory and real-world execution. Traditional training lacks a risk-free space for them to explore the "what if" scenarios of classroom management.

The Design Choice

I chose a branching scenario to give learners control over their journey. This promotes active decision-making while ensuring that the central "mentorship meeting" simulation addresses all core competencies.

Technical Features

Custom JS Triggers

Executing complex logic through Storyline triggers to track learner choices and adjust feedback dynamically.

AI-Enhanced Storyboarding

Utilizing generative AI to visualize high-fidelity classroom environments and consistent character personas.

Reflection & Iteration

Reflecting on this project, I would have loved to include even deeper branching, allowing the Preservice Teacher to face a wider variety of distinct situations directly involving their pupils.

However, the convergent model currently used effectively captures the essence of open dialogue and seeking guidance to overcome self-doubt.
