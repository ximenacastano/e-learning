About This Portfolio

Projects

This section showcases eLearning modules and demos built with industry-standard tools. You'll find a scenario-based module on teachers' mentorship and a micro-course prototype built in Articulate Storyline, a workplace learning demo for healthcare settings, a full innovation course in Articulate Rise, a folder of IT-oriented technical demos, and an interactive branching scenario — AutoFlow Sales Onboarding — where the learner plays a Sales Representative and makes five decisions that shape the outcome. The branching scenario was prototyped with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, using Claude as a design and development partner.

AI Projects

These are eLearning experiences where AI was not just a content tool but an active production partner. The AI Explanation Course is an interactive module that introduces Artificial Intelligence concepts to non-technical learners, built entirely in a Claude conversation — from instructional design to the final HTML prototype. The Sales Onboarding Course follows a full ADDIE process: needs analysis, storyboard, and interactive module, co-created with Claude across multiple design iterations.

Videos

This section showcases three video productions, each demonstrating a different AI-powered workflow. The AI Explainer Video was built with Remotion — a React-based code-driven video framework — breaking down AI concepts for teachers. The NovaSpark Corporate Video used Claude for scripting, HeyGen for AI avatar synthesis, and HyperFrames with GSAP for animated scene composition. The OSHA 29 CFR 1910 Safety Training is a 10-minute workplace compliance module produced with a fully automated Node.js pipeline: Claude authored all 8 slide scripts, ElevenLabs generated the narration, Playwright captured the HTML slides as frames, Pexels supplied b-roll footage, and FFmpeg assembled everything into the final video — triggered with a single command.
